Frequently Asked Questions

What is METAour.com?
METAour.com is a next-generation, browser-based metaverse community platform. It's designed as an accessible hub where people can connect through shared stories, ideas, immersive events, and collaborative virtual spaces — all without needing downloads or expensive VR headsets initially.
Do I need a VR headset to join?
No! METAour starts as a fully web-based experience using technologies like WebXR and A-Frame. You can explore on desktop, mobile, or tablet right in your browser. VR headset support is planned for the future to enhance immersion.
Is METAour.com free to use?
Yes, core access and community features will be free. We may introduce optional premium features or cosmetic items in the future, but the platform is built to be inclusive and open to everyone.
When will the full platform launch?
We're currently in early development with a live chat prototype at meta.metaour.com. The full metaverse hub (avatars, custom spaces, events) is targeted for launch in 2026. Sign up on the main site for updates!
How is METAour different from other metaverse platforms?
METAour emphasizes accessibility (no app downloads), community governance, user-generated content, and real human connections. We're inspired by open platforms like Decentraland but prioritize ease-of-use and browser-first experiences over heavy crypto integration initially.
Is there moderation and safety measures?
Yes — safety is a priority. We'll have community guidelines, reporting tools, moderation teams, and features like personal space bubbles to ensure a positive environment for all users.
Can I create my own spaces or events?
Absolutely! User-generated content is core to METAour. You'll be able to build custom rooms, galleries, host meetups, workshops, or art exhibitions using easy tools.
